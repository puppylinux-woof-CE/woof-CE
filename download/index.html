Download Sites

All the "official" Puppies since version 2 are hosted in iBiblio.
iBiblio also hosts the puppy specific packages (pet) used to build puppies as well as squashfs files (sfs) with kernels, kernel sources, large application and application framewarks

The iBiblio puppylinux directory is mirrored by several sites world-wide.
The NLUUG and the UoC mirrors in Europe and the AARNET and the INTERMOD mirrors in Australia are know to update regularly and are usually faster than iBiblio.
However, mirrors may take up to a day to incorporate changes in iBiblio.
